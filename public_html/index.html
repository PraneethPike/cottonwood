About

In its fiscal year, Cottonwood Compost is making strides to not only benefit the community, but to pave the way for eco-green recycling of waste. Through our hands-on projects, team-collaboration, and open atmosphere, all ideas are heard and discussed in order to carry out effective means of recycling. Cottonwood Compost has become a recognized corporation of the Inland Empire, which provides quality fertilizing materials for our agricultural community. At Cottonwood Compost we hope to make the agricultural community stronger by our product, and so we embrace critique and cooperate well with the needs of our customer.

For general questions about Cottonwood Compost, please see our FAQ page or send an email to info@cottonwoodcompost.com. Existing customers with questions about an order or subscription can email samuelcastaneda@cottonwoodcompost.com
