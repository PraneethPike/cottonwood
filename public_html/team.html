About

Cotton Wood Compost was developed from a need for a simpler food source. Creator Samuel Castaneda and team developed Soylent after recognizing the disproportionate amount of time and money they spent creating nutritionally complete meals.

Cotton Wood Compost is a food product (classified as a food, not a supplement, by the FDA) designed for use as a staple meal by all adults. Each serving of Soylent provides maximum nutrition with minimum effort.

Samuel Castaneda

Operations Manager

Samuel Castaneda is a Chemical Engineering student as well as an entrepreneur from Norco, California. In charge of operations for Cottonwood Compost, efficiency, customer care, and development of building a stronger product are of his upmost priority. Only recently added to the team in the summer of 2014, Samuel has made concessive efforts to learn all about compost and its true effect on the agricultural community.
Young, hungry, and inquisitive are words used to describe him. He currently is planning to graduate from University of California, Riverside as a Chemical Engineer in the spring of 2018. And then attempt to follow his dream of attending Stanford’s Graduate School of Business. That is…..if we can’t convince him to stay here with us. :)

Simon Attema

Owner & Partner

Proud owner of Cottonwood Dairy and Compost, Symon Attema has been considered a classic example of entrepreneurship and the American dream. Originating from the Netherlands, at a young age Symon came to America to be a pilot, and earned the highest pilot rating possible while at American Airlines. Ending an 18 year career in aviation, Symon took part in his Wife’s company, Tony’s Hay and Grain and grew the wholesale hay department of the organization. In 2013, Symon and his wife Eileen bought Cottonwood Dairy, and have since, made an impressive example of an efficient dairy with their partner Marinus Dijkstra.
Now, Symon has added another venture to his financial empire by initiating the start of Cottonwood Compost, a company focused on turning dairy waste into a valuable commodity for the agricultural community.

Marinus Dijkstra

Partner

Known as the man who can fix nearly anything, Marinus Dijkstra is the manager of Cottonwood Dairy. Originally from the Netherlands, Marinus has been involved with the dairy business for over 20 years. His current efforts of running Cottonwood Diary have been nothing short of innovation, and have stood as a model for the dairy industry altogether. Within the next decade, Marinus hopes that he may be able to expand his dairy operations, and spread the innovative thinking behind Cottonwood Dairy.
A part of his vast expertise, the development of efficient compost processing was thanks to him. Our established facilities run very effectively under Mr. Dijkstra’s system, and they will continue to improve in time, providing a better product to you the customer.
