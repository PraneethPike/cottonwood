About

Cotton Wood Compost was developed from a need for a simpler food source. Creator Samuel Castaneda and team developed Soylent after recognizing the disproportionate amount of time and money they spent creating nutritionally complete meals.

Cotton Wood Compost is a food product (classified as a food, not a supplement, by the FDA) designed for use as a staple meal by all adults. Each serving of Soylent provides maximum nutrition with minimum effort.
